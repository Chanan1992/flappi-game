Privacy Policy

This game respects your privacy. We only collect and store the following:

  • Your Pi username (to display scores on the leaderboard)
  • Your score when you play the game

We do not collect personal information such as email, phone numbers, or other sensitive data.

Your data is stored securely and is not shared with third parties. Pi transactions in the game are voluntary and only used to unlock retries or support the project through donations.

If you have questions, you can contact the developer through the Pi app.
